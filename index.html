Filing RTI in Tamil Nadu — Step by Step
1
Identify the right department
GCC for civic issues, TANGEDCO for power, CMWSSB for water, DEO for schools. Filing to wrong dept = delay.
2
Use TN State Portal
Go to rtionline.tn.gov.in for TN govt departments. For central ministries (Railways, EPFO central) use rtionline.gov.in
3
Fee: ₹0 in Tamil Nadu
Application fee is FREE for TN state departments. Central RTI: ₹10 by IPO/DD. Copy charges: ₹2/page for both.
4
Alternative: Speed Post
Write on plain paper. Address to PIO of department. Send by Speed Post. Keep tracking receipt — that\'s your proof of filing.
5
30-day reply rule
PIO must respond within 30 days. No response? File First Appeal within 30 days to the same department\'s First Appellate Authority.
6
Still no response? SIC.
File Second Appeal with Tamil Nadu State Information Commission within 90 days. Website: tnsic.tn.gov.in. PIO can be fined ₹250/day up to ₹25,000.
⚠️ TN does not yet have a single online portal for all state departments. Some departments are on rtionline.tn.gov.in — others still require Speed Post. Our AI Draft tab will tell you which path to use.
